The History of Dinosaurs

Triassic Period: 248-213 million years ago

Jurassic Period: 213-144 million years ago

Cretaceous Period: 144-65 million years ago

Tertiary Era: 65-0 million years ago

Extinction:


During the late cretaceous, around 66 million years ago, dinosaurs were thriving. Life on Earth was great, and everyone was happy. But then, an asteroid the size of Mount Everest slammmed into the gulf of mexico at 27,000 miles per hour, and the world was changed forever. The following video will give you a detailed explanation of how the poor dinosaurs went extinct, and better sheds light on the tragedy they went through.

Unfortunately, without any dinosaurs defending the world, pesky mammals arose, and eventually evolved into the humans we are today.
